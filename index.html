Ahlan Wa Sahlan!
Welcome!

Experience the magic of Wadi Rum in a Bedouin-style desert camp. Our camp is located in the heart of the desert, surrounded by towering sandstone cliffs and stunning natural beauty. You'll sleep in a traditional Bedouin tent, enjoy delicious meals cooked over an open fire, and stargaze under the clear desert sky. We offer a variety of activities, including jeep tours, camel rides, and hiking. So come and explore Wadi Rum with us, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Breathtaking Views

Explore the stunning desert landscape of Wadi Rum, Jordan. Hike among towering sandstone cliffs, climb ancient rock formations, and stargaze under the clear desert sky.

Off Road Adventures

Go jeeping, hiking, or sandboarding through the stunning desert landscape. Camp under the stars and learn about Bedouin culture.

Legendary Hospitality

Stay in a traditional Bedouin camp that offers world class hospitality. Enjoy delicious food, friendly service, and stargazing under the clear desert sky.
